Graph Transformer

GTN

Merges graph processing and neural networks . Unlike traditional CNNs, which struggle to capture complex spatial relationships , this approach models images as graphs. Each pixel or region is treated as a node, and their relationships form edges. The self-attention mechanism in the transformer allows for better understanding of both local and global dependencies within these graphs, outperforming CNNs , . This fusion leads to more precise classification and richer feature extraction .

Animal Pose

Yolo

The project aims to develop and implement a computer vision model based on YOLOv8 for horse pose estimation. The goal is to track animal behavior, enhance training in equestrian sports, and monitor the health and well-being of horses. Fine-tuning YOLOv8 involves adapting the pre-trained model to this specific task.
